- Why are some piano keys black even though their frequencies are all equally spaced in a geometric progression? Alternatively phrased as why are some notes accidentals? I understand there are different intonations, but how do they give rise to accidentals?
- What are scales? I understand it's a set of notes that can be used together, but there seems to be more to it.
- What are modes? Why are these not just scales?
- Why do note names repeat after a doubling in fundamental frequency? Why is the doubling in fundamental frequency as an interval called an octave?
- What's the key of a song? What's the source of the idea that a song is typically in a single key?
- What is the second number in a time signature supposed to represent? I am assuming the first one represents the number of beats to be grouped together so the pattern between the groups is more visible.


I think I have answers to some of these, but it's not coming together yet.
